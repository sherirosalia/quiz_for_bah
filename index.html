Where is your information?

Data from your business is everywhere, generated by all applications used to run your day-to-day operations to include business processes, mobile devices, and Internet of Things(IoT) sensors. Do you know where all this data lives and can you access it easily?

   
   
   

Who is allowed in your wheelhouse?

Do you struggle with giving access to your data to the “right” people at the right time? In other words, have you set up security permissions in a way that enables the appropriate staff to easily access the data they need to do their job?

   
   
   

Are you set up for automation?

Do you have your data stored in a way that allows for automation of tasks that typically consume hours of labor?

   
   
   

How's your cleanliness?

We think of computers as intelligent but actually they are quite stupid. Therefore, in order to analyze your data and derive the insights you need to grow your business strict naming conventions are necessary. Across all the places where your business generates data are all the columns, in a spreadsheet/table, named the same? For example, are all of the columns consistently named across all spreadsheets/tables? if not, the computer can not tell the difference between "Product ID", "Product id" and "product id" and will interpret these as completely different sources of information.

Are analyses, charts and visualizations generated by hand or is the process automated?

   
   
   

Are the powers of predictive analytics being leveraged?

Is your company producing all the forecasting reports you need to take your business to the next level, enabling the delivery of new products/services, increasing sales/profits and reducing costs?
